GLITCHLACE MEDIA

Sudo Bang Bang (2024)


ALYSS was a top influencer on THE PLATFORM until a medical incident left her with brain damage and a chronic case of the hiccups. Unable to continue her previous life, she embarks on a chaotic journey of content creation, capers for The Syndicate, and finds herself caught up in a deadly game where she's just an NPC. But, with some primo upgrades, a few friendly MiscFits, and weapons-grade willful ignorance, she just might get revenge—and with a little luck, a cure for her hiccups.
