GLITCHLACE MEDIA

Jank (2019)


Step into the temple, where the air is thick with the scent of old books and thought-sweat. Here, battlemages clash in a tournament where every Counterspell and alpha-strike matters. Follow M'Ycell, a veteran player, as he drafts a deck from jank and prepares for war. He faces off against psych-outs, mind games, and unconventional strategies, but the ultimate test comes against a skilled cheater. Feel the raw energy of a subculture where competition is life and dishonor earns a one-word sentence: "BEGONE!"
