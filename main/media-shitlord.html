GLITCHLACE MEDIA

Shitlord In the Anus of Madness (2020)


Welcome to the world of the Microbiome Revolution, where your personality, success, and social status are determined by whose shit you eat. For Shia, a former A-lister on a steady decline , life is a passive haze until he rediscovers his "vintage" 20-year-old self-produced product. The experience reboots his consciousness and leads him to the horrifying secret behind Soylent Brown Corporation's miracle supplement. In a society pacified by designer digestion, Shia becomes a lone warrior fighting a disgusting, world-altering truth that could restore humanity or get him flushed.
