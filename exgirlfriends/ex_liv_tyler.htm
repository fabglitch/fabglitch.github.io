After I wrote the screenplay for "One Night at McCools", I was invited to do the casting. There was only one girl on my mind for the role of Jewel, and that was Liv. I called her up, asked her out to dinner, and we immediately hit it off. She too was into the card game "Magic the Gathering". We had many great times together; she incinerated my elves, and I in turn, attacked her with my blastoderm on multiple occasions. Our relationship was pure bliss, unfortunately however, fate besmirched me once again, and she had to return to England to play a pixie of some sort in the upcoming feature film "Lord of the Rings."  Woe is me.
