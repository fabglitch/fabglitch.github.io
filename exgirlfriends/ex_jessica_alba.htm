\\Jessica//
When I met her, Jessica was a sweet and honest girl, so I chose to woo her affections. Eventually, she submitted to the irresistibility of my wily charms, and we started going steady. We were perhaps the most perfect couple that had ever existed, that is, until the truth came out. Jessica, like most girls, was a fan of the movie "Titanic". Sadly, this sickness was much more severe in her case. It ruined the relationship. Damn you James Cameron, damn you to hell.
