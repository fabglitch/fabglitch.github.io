\\Laetitia//
 I met Laetitia on the internet and we began talking quite frequently. She had the most intriguing personality, so I did something I had never done before, I began dating her without even having seen a picture of her. Everything was fine until I met her in Paris. As it turns out, aparently she is a world famous movie star and model. I really didn't want to be part of that scene again, so unfortunately, I had to fake my own death.
