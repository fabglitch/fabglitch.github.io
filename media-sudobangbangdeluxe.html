GLITCHLACE MEDIA

Sudo Bang Bang - Deluxe Anthology Edition (2024)


The Deluxe Edition of Fabrina Glitchlace's SUDO BANG BANG is Anthology-Level Meta-Fiction. Depending on their attention span, the reader can choose to distract themselves with a detour in a separate story that will recontextualize elements of the main narrative. In those moments the main story of Sudo Bang Bang functions as framing for an anthology.

This Choose-Your-Own-Distraction book, includes four choice Fabrina Glitchlace novellas: Shitlord In the Anus of Madness ©2017, The Zero Day Proposal ©2018, Jank ©2019, Footprints in the Worm Garden ©2020
