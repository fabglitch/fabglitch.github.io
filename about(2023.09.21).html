About Fabrina Glitchlace


Fabrina Glitchlace is an ADHD-afflicted information junkie who writes stimulating science fiction stories exploring the intersection of technology and humanity. Her vivid imagination generates compelling characters, intricate world-building, and takes readers on expeditions to the outer limits of the inner mind. Her work tackles complex themes with intelligence, heart, and humor, inviting readers to explore the infinite possibilities of the universe and the human experience.
